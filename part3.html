Igniting the Spark: My Journey Begins

Ever since I wrote my first "Hello World," I knew I didn't just want to build apps—I wanted to make a name for myself in the tech industry, standing alongside visionaries like Bill Gates and Steve Jobs. This post is my five-year roadmap to becoming a hybrid powerhouse: a Software Engineer writing high-level code, and a Cloud Engineer architecting massive virtual environments.

Building the Ladder: Skills, Knowledge, and Projects

To reach these heights, I’m combining hands-on experience with academic excellence. I plan to pursue a Master’s Degree at a top-tier university such as La Salle or Mapua while stacking certifications like AWS Solutions Architect Professional and Microsoft credentials.

My side projects—small-scale solutions for local businesses—serve as proof of innovation. Every bug, every error, and every late-night coding session pushes me closer to the engineer I aim to become.

I’ve learned that the IT world doesn’t wait. Mistakes no longer scare me; they sharpen me. Continuous effort, curiosity, and resilience will shape the professional version of myself five years from now.

Touching the Clouds: Vision for the Future

This blueprint blends education, certifications, and real-world impact. By embracing challenges and building meaningful solutions, I am positioning myself to help shape the cloud landscape in the Philippines and beyond.
